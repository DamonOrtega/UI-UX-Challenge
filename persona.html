Persona

Colleen the Collector

Profession

Owns a small graphic design business.

Technologies & Proficiencies

  • Windows and Android.
  • Has a gaming laptop and self-built gaming desktop PC.
  • Also owns many streaming and gaming devices.
  • Music nerd.
  • Tech Obsessed
  • PC gamer.
  • Collects Vinyl and vintage audio equipment.
  • Appreciates high-resolution audio.
  • Has a high sense of style.

Needs/Why She is Using Amazon

  • Familiar with the site as many purchases have been made on it.
  • Competitive pricing and free shipping as she has a Prime account.
  • Many different items with extensive reviews for item comparison.
  • She likes the wishlist feature for purchases she hasn't decided on.
  • Better pricing on Amazon when shipping is taken into account, usually.
  • Other e-commerce sites not as reliable with shipping times when compared with Amazon.
  • Amazon has better return policies.
  • Amazon tends to have more product reviews and 3rd party online tools are available to analyze products for fraudulent reviews.
  • Amazon offers free music/podcast and video streaming along with the free shipping offered by her Prime account.

Frustrations

  • Colleen is annoyed by excessive spam from e-commerce sites.
  • She does not like to wait long for purchases to arrive at her doorstep.
  • She does not like dealing with customer support when a product fails or does not meet her needs. Especially if a phone call is required.
  • Collen hates when websites have limited pictures of the products she is interested in.
  • Things must be pleasing to her eye, she disapproves of what she considers bad design.
